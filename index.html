Bonchon Chicken

Serving fried chicken to die for

Championing the craft of golden fried chicken, Bonchon's chicken wings famously grant immortality while the chicken drumsticks endow the consumer with an out-of-body experience through the universe and beyond. Celebrities revived from the wings: Oprah Winfrey, Mick Jagger, and Kristen Bell. Bonchon currently holds a 3.5/5.0 rating on Yelp.
